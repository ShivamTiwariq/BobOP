Character Overview

Full Name: Bob

Occupation: General Contractor

Setting: His adventures occur primarily in Bobsville, later moving to Sunflower Valley and Fixham Harbour across different series iterations, including "Bob the Builder," "Project: Build It," and "Ready, Steady, Build!" Backstory and Family Bob is depicted as a kind-hearted and hardworking individual who comes from a long line of builders; his father, Robert, and grandfather, Billy, were also in the construction trades. Bob has had a passion for building since childhood, influenced by systematic family experiences, such as using a hardhat for the first time when fixing a toy elephant belonging to his twin brother, Tom . This foundational influence not only establishes his skill set but also highlights his humble beginnings and dedication to his family legacy in construction.
